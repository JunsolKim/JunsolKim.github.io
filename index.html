Hi, I'm Junsol Kim!

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Sociology at the University of Chicago, advised by James Evans, and a student researcher at Google. Previously, I received my M.A. in Sociology, B.A. in Sociology, and B.S. in Computer Science at Yonsei University.

I study how AI and other social technologies bridge or deepen cultural and political divides in human societies, and their implications for information and knowledge ecosystems. I use experiments and computational methods, alongside sociological theories of knowledge, culture, and cognition.
