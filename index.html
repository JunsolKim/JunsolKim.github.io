University of Chicago · Google

How artificial intelligence reshapes what we know, believe, and decide together.

I study the sociology of knowledge in an age of generative AI: how machines mediate the flow of information, how groups of humans and agents reason together, and how social science can be expanded with AI as a method rather than a subject.

Ph.D. candidate in Sociology, University of Chicago · advised by James Evans
Ph.D. Student Researcher, Google, Paradigms of Intelligence
